Ludwig van Beethoven



A classical German composer and pianist who learned under Joseph Hayden, he is often considered to be the greatest composer of all time. In the later years of his life began to suffer from progressive deafness, yet somehow it miraculously did not stop him from producing some of his most important works even when he could barely hear.
